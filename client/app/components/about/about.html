About Academic Life Helper

The Academic Life Helper (ALH for short), is a tool aimed at helping students keep focus on what's important, providing an all-inclusive dashboard that integrates with Moodle and helps you stay on top of all your exams, assignments, or extra-curricular activities!

ALH comes with a variety of functionalities to help you succeed!

Simple

Everything is set so that you can easily get in and find whatever you're looking for, no unnecessary steps or tedious login screens

Flexible

We've been very careful at making sure you can access and use AademicLifeHelper on any website, device, or format you need

Useful

Taking a no-nonsense approach to development, we're providing the most important information you want first, and the details come later
